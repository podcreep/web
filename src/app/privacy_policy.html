Privacy policy

Codeka Consulting is the developer of Pod Creep and we have adopted this Privacy Policy in order to inform you of our intentions with respect to information collected from you in the course of using the app and website. Your use of the app and/or this website constitutes your acceptance of this Privacy Policy.

When you use the app or this website, certain information is automatically logged including, but not limited to, information about the device you are using, such as model number, device identification number, and IP address. In addition, in order to be able to log in, you must provide an email address and password.

In particular, this email address and password is considered Strictly Confidential and will never be shared with third parties. Your password is stored hashed on our servers, but still, we strongly recommend you do not share passwords (use a different password for every site you use, include ours).

Additionally, we will never contact you by email (unless you contact us first, say for the purposes of support).

Finally, the app and this website store various information required for basic functionality of a podcast app. In particular, we store:

  • Which podcasts you are subscribed to
  • How far you have listened to each epsiode of each podcast
  • Which episodes you have finished listening
  • Which episode you are currently listening to

We store this information so that it can be synced between devices.

Cancelling

If you wish to cancel your account, you can email dean@codeka.com at any time and all information, as described above, will be permanently purged from our servers.
